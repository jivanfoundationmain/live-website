Since 2018

Empowering Society Through Education, Service & Social Responsibility

Established in 2018, Jivan Foundation is a dedicated non-governmental organization working towards the holistic development and empowerment of society through education, social welfare initiatives, youth development, and community engagement.

With a vision to create equal opportunities for every individual, the foundation actively supports students, women, underprivileged communities, and economically weaker sections of society by providing guidance, awareness, and opportunities for growth.

Jivan Foundation strongly believes that education and social awareness are the most powerful tools for transforming lives. The organization continuously works to promote quality education, skill development, personality enhancement, leadership building, and community service among the youth.

Our Purpose

Vision, Mission & Aim

👁️

Our Vision

To build an educated, empowered, and socially responsible society where every individual gets equal opportunities for growth, learning, and success through education, awareness, and humanity.

🎯

Our Mission

To promote quality education, youth empowerment, skill development, social awareness, and community welfare through seminars, training programs, social initiatives, and cultural activities while inspiring positive change and leadership among students and society.

🚀

Our Aim

To empower society through education, youth development, social awareness, and community welfare by creating opportunities that inspire learning, leadership, and positive social change.

What We Do

Our Social Initiatives

  • 🏥 Health Check-up Camps – Free health screenings for students and community members
  • 🩸 Blood Donation Drives – Life-saving campaigns promoting voluntary blood donation
  • 🚦 Traffic Awareness Programs – Road safety education for communities
  • 🧹 Swachh Bharat & Cleanliness Drives – Hygiene and sanitation campaigns
  • 🌳 Tree Plantation Drives – 500+ saplings planted across Maharashtra
  • 🧘 Yoga & Wellness Awareness – District and national-level yoga workshops
  • 📚 Educational Seminars & Workshops – Career guidance on UPSC, MPSC, SSC
  • 🎒 Study Kits & Uniform Distribution – Support for ZP school students
  • 🏠 Orphanage & Community Support – Clothes distribution and welfare activities
  • 🤝 NSS Volunteer Activities – Active NSS unit coordination and leadership camps
Our Journey

Foundation Timeline

2018
Foundation Established

Jivan Foundation was established with a mission to empower society through education and social welfare.

2019
NSS Collaboration Begins

Partnership with NSS units of SST College begins, amplifying community reach through student volunteers.

2021
Blood Donation & Health Camps

First large-scale blood donation drive and health check-up camps organized serving hundreds of beneficiaries.

2023
Expanded Programs (2023–24)

400+ trees planted, national webinars on competitive exams, yoga workshops, education enrichment at slum areas.

2024
Scaling Up (2024–25)

Sankalp Siddhi yoga with 400+ participants, district Surya Namaskar with 300+ volunteers, government scheme awareness in Rahatoli village.

2025
Future Plans

Expanding reach to more districts, launching digital literacy programs, setting up community learning centres, and strengthening women empowerment initiatives.
