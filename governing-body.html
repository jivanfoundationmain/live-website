President's Message

Mr. Jeevan Pandurang Vichare
President, Jivan Foundation
NSS District Coordinator, Thane
Vice Principal, SST College, Ulhasnagar

Dear Visitors,

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to Jivan Foundation.

Since our establishment in 2018, our foundation has been committed to serving society through education, social awareness, youth empowerment, and community welfare activities. We firmly believe that meaningful social transformation begins with education, compassion, and collective responsibility.

At Jivan Foundation, our mission is not only to support individuals in need but also to inspire confidence, self-reliance, leadership, and social values among the younger generation. Through various educational initiatives, awareness programs, health camps, environmental drives, and welfare activities, we continuously strive to make a positive impact on society.

We are proud to work alongside dedicated volunteers, educators, NSS students, and social workers who contribute selflessly towards our shared vision of creating an empowered and progressive society.

Every small effort towards education, health, environment, and humanity creates a stronger foundation for the future. We remain committed to expanding our reach and continuing our service with honesty, dedication, and social responsibility.

I sincerely thank all our supporters, volunteers, students, and well-wishers for their continuous encouragement and cooperation. Together, let us work towards building a better, educated, and compassionate society.

Warm Regards, Mr. Jeevan Pandurang Vichare  |  President, Jivan Foundation
