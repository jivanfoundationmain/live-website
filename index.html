About Jivan Foundation

A perfect way to shape your life...

Jivan Foundation envisions creating a premier educational institution that delivers high-quality, accessible, and skill-oriented education. We bridge educational gaps, especially for students from tribal, OBC, and middle-class communities, while preparing them for both higher education and direct employment opportunities.

Our initiative aligns with the "Skill India" and "Make in India" movements, ensuring that local talent is nurtured into skilled manpower contributing to national development.

Read Full Story

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing and intellectually stimulating environment that fosters academic excellence with equal access for diverse socio-economic groups. We offer career-oriented, self-financing, and job-relevant courses while developing students into lifelong learners, ethical leaders, and skilled professionals.

Our Vision

To be a premier institution that nurtures innovation, inclusivity, and lifelong learning, empowering students to excel globally while serving locally. We aspire to become a model institution that shapes employable graduates equipped with 21st-century skills and contributes to regional socio-economic development.
