Who We Are

About Jivan Foundation

Established in 2018, Jivan Foundation is a dedicated NGO working towards holistic development and empowerment through education, social welfare, youth development, and community engagement.

Educational Support

Providing quality education, career guidance, and scholarships to students from underprivileged backgrounds.

Social Welfare

Health camps, blood donation drives, clothes distribution, and humanitarian support for vulnerable communities.

Youth Empowerment

Skill development, personality enhancement, leadership training, and NSS volunteer programs for youth.

Community Development

Tree plantation, cleanliness drives, yoga awareness, and government scheme outreach in rural areas.

Our Purpose

Vision, Mission & Values

Our Vision

To build an educated, empowered, and socially responsible society where every individual gets equal opportunities for growth, learning, and success through education, awareness, and humanity.

Our Mission

To promote quality education, youth empowerment, skill development, social awareness, and community welfare through seminars, training programs, social initiatives, and cultural activities while inspiring positive change and leadership.

Our Aim

To empower society through education, youth development, social awareness, and community welfare by creating opportunities that inspire learning, leadership, and positive social change.

Core Values

Integrity & Transparency Education & Empowerment Inclusivity & Equality Innovation & Creativity Social Responsibility Leadership & Service Compassion & Humanity
What We Do

Our Activities

🏥
Health Check-up Camps

Free health screenings, blood pressure monitoring, and general health awareness for students, staff, and community members.

🩸
Blood Donation Drives

Regular blood donation camps saving lives and promoting a culture of voluntary donation among youth.

🌳
Tree Plantation Drives

Hundreds of saplings planted annually in forest areas and community spaces to promote environmental sustainability.

🧘
Yoga Awareness Camps

District and university-level yoga workshops promoting physical, mental, and spiritual well-being.

📚
Educational Support

Study kits, uniform distribution for ZP school students, and educational enrichment for underprivileged children.

🚦
Traffic Awareness Programs

Community outreach and awareness drives promoting road safety and responsible traffic behaviour.

🧹
Cleanliness Drives

Swachh Bharat-inspired cleanliness campaigns in schools, villages and public spaces.

🎯
Career Guidance Sessions

Webinars and workshops on UPSC, MPSC, SSC, Railway exams empowering students to achieve their career goals.

🤝
NSS Volunteer Activities

Active NSS unit coordination, leadership camps, government scheme awareness, and rural community engagement.

Leadership

Chairperson's Message

"

Education and social awareness are the most powerful tools for transforming lives. At Jivan Foundation, we believe that every individual deserves equal opportunities to learn, grow, and succeed with dignity.

Dear Visitors,
It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to Jivan Foundation. Since our establishment in 2018, our foundation has been committed to serving society through education, social awareness, youth empowerment, and community welfare activities. We firmly believe that meaningful social transformation begins with education, compassion, and collective responsibility. At Jivan Foundation, our mission is not only to support individuals in need but also to inspire confidence, self-reliance, leadership, and social values among the younger generation. Through various educational initiatives, awareness programs, health camps, environmental drives, and welfare activities, we continuously strive to make a positive impact on society. We are proud to work alongside dedicated volunteers, educators, NSS students, and social workers who contribute selflessly towards our shared vision of creating an empowered and progressive society. Every small effort towards education, health, environment, and humanity creates a stronger foundation for the future. We remain committed to expanding our reach and continuing our service with honesty, dedication, and social responsibility. I sincerely thank all our supporters, volunteers, students, and well-wishers for their continuous encouragement and cooperation. Together, let us work towards building a better, educated, and compassionate society.

Warm Regards,
President
Shri. Jeevan Manjula Pandurang Vichare
