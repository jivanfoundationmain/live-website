About Jivan Foundation

Empowering communities through accessible, quality education and lifelong learning since our founding in Kalyan, Maharashtra.

Our Story

Shaping Lives Through Education

The Jivan Foundation envisions creating an educational hub that delivers high-quality, accessible, and skill-oriented education. We aim to bridge educational gaps, especially for students from tribal, OBC, and middle-class communities.

Our initiative aligns with the "Skill India" and "Make in India" movements, ensuring that local talent is nurtured into skilled manpower contributing to national development.

Based in Kalyan, Maharashtra, we believe every student deserves access to quality education regardless of their socio-economic background.

Vision, Mission & Aim

Everything we do is guided by a clear purpose — to make quality education accessible to all.

Our Vision

To be a premier institution that nurtures innovation, inclusivity, and lifelong learning — empowering students to excel globally while serving locally.

Our Mission

  • Nurturing and intellectually stimulating environment
  • Academic excellence with equal access for all
  • Career-oriented, job-relevant courses
  • Lifelong learners and ethical leaders

Our Aim

To establish a premier institution promoting academic excellence, social equity, and career-oriented learning — producing competent, ethical, and socially responsible citizens.

Our Journey

Long-Term Roadmap

A phased approach to building Maharashtra's premier educational institution.

Phase 1

Establish & Grow

  • Junior College with all three streams (Arts, Commerce, Science)
  • Strong NSS and social outreach programs
  • Night college feasibility study for working students
  • Degree college courses: CS, IT, AI, BMS, BAF, BBI, Mass Media
  • Industry partnerships for internships and placements
Phase 2

Expand & Innovate

  • Postgraduate programs and research initiatives
  • Incubation centre for student startups
  • Distance learning programs for wider reach
  • NAAC accreditation pursuit
  • Green campus with solar, water harvesting and ERP
