Why Volunteer With Us?

Volunteering with Jivan Foundation is about more than giving back — it's about being part of a movement that transforms lives. Whether you're a professional, a student, or a retiree, your skills and time are invaluable to us.

Opportunities

How You Can Help

Guest Lecturer

Share your industry expertise with students through one-off or regular sessions on your area of specialization.

Student Mentor

Provide one-on-one guidance to help students with career decisions, skill development and personal growth.

Tech Volunteer

Help us build and maintain our digital infrastructure — websites, ERP systems, and computer lab support.

Event Support

Help organise and run events — from cultural fests to competitive exam coaching drives and NSS camps.

Outreach & Awareness

Help us reach more students in rural and semi-urban areas through community awareness and enrolment drives.

Fundraising Support

Help us secure more scholarships and resources by connecting us with potential donors and corporate sponsors.
